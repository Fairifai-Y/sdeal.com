Privacy Policy

Plain Language Summary

This summary provides a quick overview of how SDeal handles your personal data. We only use your information when necessary, such as for delivering your order, sending updates, or improving our platform. We never sell your data. You can always ask to see, correct or delete your data. For full details, please read the sections below.

Consent and Cookie Management

SDeal uses cookies and other technologies for website functionality, analytics, and advertising. Before we place cookies that are not strictly necessary, we request your explicit consent via a cookie banner. You can manage or withdraw your consent at any time via the cookie settings.

Retention Periods

SDeal retains personal data no longer than necessary for the purpose it was collected. As a guideline:

  • Order data: kept for 7 years (tax requirement)
  • Account data: kept until the user deletes the account
  • Newsletter subscriptions: kept until user unsubscribes
  • Job applications: kept for 4 weeks after rejection, unless consented for longer

Privacy Contact Person

Although SDeal does not have a formal Data Protection Officer (DPO), our Privacy Coordinator handles all questions and complaints regarding your personal data. You can contact us via cs@sdeal.com.

Updated Legal Reference

The Dutch Data Protection Act (Wbp) has been replaced by the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). For more information, visit the official Dutch Data Protection Authority site at autoriteitpersoonsgegevens.nl.

SDeal values the protection of your personal data. In this Privacy Statement, we aim to provide clear and transparent information on how we handle your personal data. We do everything possible to safeguard your privacy and handle personal data with care. SDeal complies with all applicable laws and regulations, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). This means we will at least:

  • Process your personal data in accordance with the purpose for which they were provided. These purposes and types of personal data are described in this Privacy Statement;
  • Limit the processing of your personal data to only those data that are minimally required for the purposes for which they are processed;
  • Request your explicit consent if we need it for the processing of your personal data;
  • Take appropriate technical and organizational measures to ensure the security of your personal data;
  • Not transfer personal data to other parties, unless necessary for the purposes for which they were provided;
  • Cooperate with the rights concerning your personal data and inform you of these rights.

When an agreement is made between you and a third party on our platform, this third party will process your personal data for handling your order. Please refer to the privacy statement of that third party or contact them for more information on how your data is handled.

Legal Grounds for Processing Personal Data

Under the law, SDeal may only process personal data if there is a legal basis. SDeal uses the following legal grounds to process personal data. We process your data:

  • In connection with the establishment and/or execution of the purchase agreement between you and SDeal;
  • Because we are legally obligated to do so;
  • With your consent;
  • Because we (or a third party) have a legitimate interest. Our legitimate interests are described in this privacy policy.

Purposes for Processing Personal Data

SDeal processes your personal data for, among others, the following purposes:

Improving Services

To provide the best possible service, we use your data for analysis, contact, and possibly surveys. We analyze click behavior and newsletter activity to optimize our content.

Account

Your personal data (e.g., name, address, phone) are stored in your "My SDeal" account. This helps you avoid entering your details repeatedly and speeds up our service in case of questions.

Newsletter

You can sign up for our newsletter, which may be personalized. You can unsubscribe anytime via the link at the bottom of each email.

Reviews

When you write a review, it helps other customers. These are stored in your account.

Advertisements

We process IP address, cookie ID, social ID, and browsing behavior to display relevant ads, including through social media targeting. We don’t share personal data with third parties unless you consent.

Partners

When you buy from a third party on our platform, they process your data. See their privacy policy for details.

Promotions

If you win a prize, we’ll contact you using your provided data.

Recruitment

When you apply for a job, we collect your contact details to get in touch about your application.

Fraud Prevention

We may use customer data to investigate fraud and may share it with authorities.

We may collect: name, address, ZIP code, city, phone number, email, date of birth, payment data, gender.

Special Categories of Data

SDeal does not intend to collect special categories of data such as those regarding political views, race, religion, health, or criminal records. If we do, we’ll ask for your explicit consent.

Sharing with Third Parties

We only share data when necessary for the agreement or when legally required. We only share with parties we have processor agreements with. We do not share data outside the EU.

Minors

We only process data from minors (under 16) with written parental or guardian consent.

Retention Period

We do not store your data longer than necessary unless the law requires otherwise.

Cookies

Cookies enhance your experience. They remember settings, enable login, store your cart, and allow us to analyze visits.

Functional Cookies

Used for sessions, cart, and login preferences.

Analytics Cookies

Google Analytics, New Relic: for visitor statistics. You can opt out via your browser or specific plugins.

Advertising Cookies

Used by Google Ads and Facebook to show relevant ads. You can opt out on their platforms.

Security

We take measures like encrypted backups, user access controls, and staff training to protect your data.

Data Breaches

SDeal has a protocol for data breaches and handles them with care.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, delete, object to processing, or transfer your data. We may ask for identification.

Accuracy

You guarantee the accuracy of your data. SDeal may deny access in case of false or incomplete data.

Complaints

If you have a complaint, contact us. You also have the right to file a complaint with the Dutch Data Protection Authority (Autoriteit Persoonsgegevens).
